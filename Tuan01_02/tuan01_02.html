This book is about a lawyer who begins revaluating his prorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well writen and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Film, The Pelican Belief, and The Cilent
